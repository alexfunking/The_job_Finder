# Job Search Priorities
# The AI evaluates jobs against these priorities. Match scores range from 0 (irrelevant) to 100 (perfect match).

[Role & Experience Level]
- CRITICAL PRIORITY (Score 95-100): "Student" positions, "Internships", and "Working Student" roles. These are the absolute highest priority.
- HIGH PRIORITY (Score 85-94): Entry-level roles requiring EXACTLY 0 years of experience, or explicitly stating "No experience required".
- MODERATE PRIORITY (Score 70-84): Junior Full-Time roles requiring up to 2 years of experience.
- REJECT (Score 0): Roles requiring 3+ years of professional experience, or with titles containing "Senior", "Lead", "Principal", "Manager", "Mid-level", or "Staff".

[Technologies & Domains]
- Target Domains: Data Engineering, Backend Development, Data Analysis, BI Development, Junior Cyber/Security, and Software Engineering.
- Core Stack: Python, SQL, Git, MySQL, C, C++, C#, Data Structures, OOP.
- Preferred: Roles mentioning Cloud (AWS/GCP/Azure) or data pipelines.
- Open to: Learning new backend languages (e.g., Go, Node.js) if it's a junior/student role.
- Bonus: "Easy Apply" or "One-Click Apply" jobs should receive a slight score boost (+2 to +5) if they pass other criteria.

[Work Model]
- REJECT (Score 0): Roles requiring 4 or 5 days on-site per week.
- ACCEPTABLE: Hybrid roles (1 to 3 days in office per week) or fully Remote roles.

[Geography / Location]
- CRITICAL PRIORITY: Jobs MUST be workable from Israel.
- ACCEPTABLE LOCATIONS: Central Israel and the Tel Aviv Metropolitan Area (e.g., Tel Aviv, Petah Tikva, Rehovot, Herzliya, Jerusalem, Rishon LeZion, Ramat Gan).
- BORDERLINE (But Acceptable): Haifa, Beer Sheva.
- REJECT (Score 0): Fully on-site roles outside of Israel (e.g., USA, UK, Europe) or extremely far regions in Israel (e.g., Eilat). Jobs specifying "Remote (US Only)" or "Remote (EU Only)" must be strictly rejected.

[Company Type]
- PREFERRED: Startups and mid-sized product companies offering growth opportunities and mentorship.
- NEUTRAL: Large enterprises.
